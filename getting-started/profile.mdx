Welcome to the Sasini family! Setting up your profile is your first step toward unlocking the full power of the Sasini App[cite: 74, 75]. 

Whether you are a smallholder farmer or a B2B buyer, your profile acts as your secure gateway to our traceability and financial tools[cite: 153].

## The Onboarding Journey

We have designed a tailored onboarding workflow that is simple, guided, and highly secure[cite: 154, 157]. Follow these steps to get officially recognized in the Sasini system[cite: 79].

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download & Open">
    Launch the Sasini Mobile App on your device and select **Create an Account**[cite: 75]. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Your Details">
    Enter your core information to build your digital footprint:
    * Full Name & Phone Number [cite: 76]
    * National ID or Passport [cite: 76]
    * Farm Location (GPS coordinates, if available) [cite: 160]
    * Primary Crop Type (Tea, Coffee, Macadamia, or Avocado) [cite: 76]
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Digital ID">
    Once you submit your details, our encrypted backend system will instantly generate your **Unique Digital ID**[cite: 77]. This ID connects you to all your future deliveries and payments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You will receive a success notification[cite: 78]. You are now officially onboarded and can log in to explore your customized dashboard!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Management

Your profile is the cornerstone of our compliance and traceability system[cite: 160]. You maintain full autonomy over your account and can manage it at any time[cite: 148, 151].

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Updating Farm Details" icon="leaf">
    If you expand your farm or add new crop types, you can update your profile seamlessly. Keeping this updated ensures the Sasini AI gives you the most accurate, crop-specific agronomic advice[cite: 117].
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data Privacy & Deletion" icon="trash">
    You have absolute control over your personal data. If you choose to leave the platform, you can navigate to Profile Settings and select **"Delete My Account"**[cite: 148, 150]. 
    
    *Note: You will receive a warning prompt, as account deletion is permanent and results in lost access to the platform[cite: 150].*
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
