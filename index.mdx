Welcome to the official documentation for the **Sasini Mobile Application**. 

This platform is a unified digital ecosystem designed for Sasini's core business areas, including tea, coffee, avocado, macadamia, and retail. It serves as your central hub, connecting farmers, buyers, and management to enhance operational efficiency and transparency.

## What is the Sasini App?

The Sasini App is built to empower over 30,000 smallholder farmers, B2B buyers, and everyday consumers by placing the power of modern agricultural technology right in your pocket. 

Whether you are tracking produce deliveries, ordering farm inputs, or asking the Sasini AI for crop advice, this app is designed to make your farming and business experience seamless.
