The Sasini App transforms how deliveries are logged and tracked. By digitizing the weighbridge process, farmers and B2B buyers have absolute, real-time visibility into their produce metrics, eliminating paper trails and reducing discrepancies.

## The Delivery Lifecycle

When a farmer arrives at a Sasini collection center or factory, the process is heavily automated to ensure speed and accuracy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Arrival & Identification">
    The farmer presents their **Unique Digital ID** (via QR code or Farmer ID number) at the collection center. The clerk scans it, instantly pulling up the farmer's profile on the centralized Sasini system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Weighbridge Integration">
    The produce (Tea, Coffee, Macadamia, or Avocado) is weighed. The digital scale is integrated directly with the Sasini backend, automatically pushing the exact <Tooltip tip="Measured in Kilograms (kg) to two decimal places.">net weight</Tooltip> to the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant Push Notification">
    The moment the weight is logged, the farmer receives an instant push notification on their Sasini Mobile App confirming the delivery.

## Interactive Delivery Dashboard

The user interface is designed to be highly visual, using our vibrant `#76BD22` green to highlight successful deliveries and positive metrics.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Farmer View (Mobile App)">
    Farmers can view a beautifully visualized history of all deliveries. 
    * **Daily Logs:** Shows exact time, date, and weight of each drop-off.
    * **Monthly Aggregates:** A dynamic chart showing total yield for the current harvest season.
    * **Quality Grades:** If applicable (e.g., Macadamia nut quality), the grade is displayed alongside the weight.

## Technical Details: The Data Payload

For administrators and developers, understanding exactly what data is captured during a delivery is crucial. Below is the exact data structure generated the moment produce hits the scale.

<Info>
  **Data Integrity:** All delivery logs are immutable. Once a delivery is synced to the backend, it cannot be altered by the farmer or the local clerk, preventing tampering.
</Info>

<ParamField path="delivery_id" type="string" required>
  A unique, encrypted 16-character alphanumeric string generated for every single drop-off.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="farmer_id" type="string" required>
  The Unique Digital ID of the farmer making the delivery.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="produce_type" type="enum" required>
  Must be one of: `tea`, `coffee`, `macadamia`, `avocado`.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="net_weight_kg" type="float" required>
  The final weight of the produce, strictly verified by the automated scale.
</ParamField>
<ParamField path="timestamp" type="ISO 8601 Date" required>
  The exact time the weight was recorded to the millisecond.
</ParamField>
