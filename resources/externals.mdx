## 1. Farmer Training Content

Farming techniques are always evolving. To ensure our farmers are achieving the highest possible yields with the best quality, we provide a rich library of multimedia training content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Video Tutorials">
    Access high-quality video masterclasses produced by Sasini's top agronomists. Learn visual techniques for pruning tea bushes, picking perfect coffee cherries, or managing soil erosion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Downloadable Manuals">
    Need offline access? You can download PDF agronomy guides directly to your device so you can read them while out in the field without an internet connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<div style={{ height: '20px' }} />

<Tip>
  **Continuous Learning:** Whenever you complete a training module, it is logged in your profile. Farmers who actively participate in Sasini training programs often receive priority access to subsidized farm inputs!
</Tip>

<div style={{ height: '20px' }} />

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Backend Security: Global CDN Video Streaming" icon="server">
    **Under the Hood:** High-definition video can quickly drain a farmer's mobile data. To solve this, our training videos are hosted on a Content Delivery Network (CDN) like AWS CloudFront. The app automatically detects the user's internet speed and adjusts the video resolution dynamically (Adaptive Bitrate Streaming) to prevent buffering and save data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 2. Sasini E-Commerce (Retail Shop)

Did you know you can buy the very products you help create? The app provides a direct gateway to Sasini's consumer retail store.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Premium Products">
    Tap the **E-Commerce** link to seamlessly browse our catalog of finished, export-quality Tea, Coffee, and Macadamia nuts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Exclusive Farmer Discounts">
    As a registered Sasini farmer, your app authentication token automatically applies a special internal discount at checkout for all retail purchases.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<div style={{ height: '20px' }} />

<Info>
  **Delivery Note:** Retail products purchased through the E-Commerce portal are shipped via standard consumer couriers, not our heavy factory logistics fleet. Delivery times and fees may vary based on your location.
</Info>

<div style={{ height: '20px' }} />

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Backend Security: Secure OAuth Routing" icon="server">
    **Under the Hood:** The app doesn't force the user to log in twice. When navigating to the E-Commerce store (which may run on a separate platform like Shopify or WooCommerce), the backend passes a secure OAuth token. This creates a frictionless "Single Sign-On" (SSO) experience while keeping payment gateways completely isolated and secure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
