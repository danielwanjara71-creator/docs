At Sasini, the privacy and security of your data are our top priorities. Whether you are a smallholder farmer tracking your payments or a B2B buyer placing a bulk order, the Sasini App uses state-of-the-art security measures to keep your information safe.

## Unique Digital IDs

When you register on the Sasini App, you are assigned a **Unique Digital ID**. 

This ID acts as your secure digital signature across the entire platform. It ensures that:
* **Your Deliveries are Yours:** Every kilogram of tea, coffee, macadamia, or avocado you deliver is permanently linked to your specific ID.
* **Accurate Payments:** Financial calculations and payment receipts are routed exclusively to the account tied to your Digital ID, preventing errors or fraud.
* **Traceability:** Buyers can trace produce back to the source, ensuring transparency without compromising your personal, private data.

## End-to-End Encryption

[cite_start]The Sasini App is highly secure, utilizing advanced **end-to-end encryption**[cite: 203]. 
* [cite_start]All data transmitted between your mobile device and the Sasini backend servers is encrypted[cite: 203]. 
* This means that your personal profile, payment details, and farm inputs are protected from unauthorized access at all times.

## Role-Based Access Control

[cite_start]The app uses Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to ensure users only see what they are supposed to see[cite: 155]. 
* **Farmers** only have access to their own farm data, deliveries, and payment histories.
* **Sasini Management** and **Extension Officers** have secure, restricted access to assist you and manage supply chain logistics.
* Your account settings allow you to manage your profile securely, including options to change your password or permanently deactivate your account if needed. 

<Tip>
**Keep your account safe:** Never share your password or your Unique Digital ID with anyone. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact the Help Center immediately.
</Tip>
